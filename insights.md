# Analysis Insights

## Overall Usage
The London Underground handles over 4.8 million daily journeys, demonstrating its importance as a primary transportation system.

## Peak Travel Patterns
The PM Peak period (4–7 PM) experiences the highest traffic, likely due to commuters returning home from work.

## Zone-Based Trends
- Zone 1 accounts for over half of all journeys
- Outer zones (2–5) show more trips originating from home
- Central London (Zone 1) has more work-related origins

## Travel Purpose Insights
- Work is the dominant reason for travel
- Home and work remain top purposes across all zones
- Travel patterns align with typical commuting behavior

## Tourism Patterns
Tourism-related journeys are more evenly distributed throughout the day, unlike commuter traffic which is concentrated in peak hours.

## Key Takeaway
London’s transit system is heavily commuter-driven, but tourism adds consistent demand across all time periods, requiring balanced scheduling strategies.
